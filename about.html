It's just me

My name is Grace. I am just an ordinary person with extraordinary love for arts and music. I hold a job that compel me to go to work 8 hours a day, 5 days a week. I took arts and music as my pastime and hobby. It's one way of destressing myself after a busy and hectic week.

My drawings are just simple. I love to draw my pets, animals and nature. My favorite drawing materials are pencils and colored pencils. Though I made some watercolor drawings but I've found it kinda hard.

I do also love to sing. For me, music is a language where you can express your emotions. Music uplifts mood. Through music, it can defuse and unpleasant state.

I expressed myself through my arts and music. My love for them is deep. Forever this would be part of my existence.

"With music and art, I can create the kind of world in which I wish to live" -- Joseph Curiale
